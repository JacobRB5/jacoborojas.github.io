In summary, when you create a piece of creative content, such as art or illustrations or music, you legally own the rights to use that piece of art as you wiil. This makes it so that if someone puts some David Guetta music over their tutorial video, the author of said peace can ask for your music to be removed and/or ask for part of the revenues.
